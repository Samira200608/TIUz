Each region of our country, not only with its natural climate, but also with its rich historical heritage, unique customs, lifestyle and even unique traditions, never repeats itself. That is why the number of tourists from all over the world who want to see this country and breathe its air is growing every year. In particular, local and foreign guests from all regions of Uzbekistan with high potential in the field of tourism, modern infrastructure.

The nature of Uzbekistan is very beautiful and rich in beautiful places, and its mountainous areas, such as Shohimardon, Bakhmal, Chimgan, in some sense, are rightly compared to the Swiss nature. In spring and summer, a day of rest in the nature of Omonkuton, Agalik and Sarmish gives a person a lot of spiritual and physical nourishment. The nature of deserts and valleys in the republic is also unique. They serve as a source of spiritual and material wealth for man.
